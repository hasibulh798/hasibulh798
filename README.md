## Why Work With Me

**Clean Architecture** — I structure applications so they remain easy to extend and maintain as they grow, avoiding technical debt from day one.

**API Design** — I build REST APIs that are predictable, well-documented, and easy for teams to integrate with.

**Performance** — I optimize for real-world load, not just development-environment benchmarks.

**Security** — Authentication, authorization, and data handling are treated as first-class concerns, not afterthoughts.

**Scalable Solutions** — Systems are designed to handle growth in users, data, and features without requiring a rewrite.

**Responsive Communication** — Clear updates, realistic timelines, and fast turnaround on questions — critical for remote collaboration across time zones.

I work well with startups that need to move fast, agencies that need reliable delivery, and businesses that need software they can depend on.
